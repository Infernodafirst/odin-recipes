Ramen Recipe:

Description:

This recipe is about the world famous japanese dish known as the Ramen. Ramen is a healthy and delicious meal.

It can take days to prepare Ramen.

Ingredients:

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Pork
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chilli Pepper
  • Sesame
  • Spring Onions

Steps:

  1. Make Pork Broth
  2. Boil the Ramen Noodles
  3. Half Boil the Eggs
  4. Prepare the Pork
  5. Add all the cooked ingredients and garnish with spring onions
