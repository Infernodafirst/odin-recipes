Pizza Recipe:

Description:

Pizza is a very popular Italian dish. Just like the sushi, Pizza has also been Americanized and thus made unhealthy. This recipe however, will focus on the healhty Italian Pizza.

This is not an attack to American people. They can live and eat however they want.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps:

  1. Flatten the pizza dough to be a flat large circle
  2. Add cheese on top of the dough
  3. Add the toppings of your choice on the pizza
  4. Bake in an oven until ready
